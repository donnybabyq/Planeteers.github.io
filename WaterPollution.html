CAUSES OF THE PROBLEM

One of the causes of throwing garbages in water forms is that the youth are too lazy to throw their garbages in trash bins.

After they are done eating, they'll throw it to a near waterforms if ever there are some near them.

Lack of trash bins are also one of the reason, whenever they are on roads or parks.

Whenever youth have garbages on hands, they do not want to leave it in their bags so what they do is either throw it or leave it anywhere. With lack of garbage collectors, people are forced to throw their garbages in rivers or other waterforms.

Usability Test Results

2 out of 6 of our participants said that their prior knowledge about our topic isn't wide enough or in average only. While 2 out of 6 said that their knowledge is wide enough. 4 out of 6 of our participants said that youth throwing garbages in water forms is very relevant to the environmental issue. Most of our participants said that if we solve the issue of the students throwing, it will help us have a better environment. Participants also stated that they usually see students throwing garbages in waterforms near their houses. Because of lack of trash bins in roads or parks.

4 out of 6 said that our prepared solution is detailed, informative and easy to understand. They also said that our animationcathes their attention, and the pictures, informations are connected with each other. They also said that our prototype is presentable enough.

4 out of 6 of the participants said that they feel bad about this environmental issues happening today. Our participants have no suggestions to improve both prototype and animation. Participants stated that as a part of youth they would able to help in solving the issue by avoiding throwing garbage in bodies of water.
